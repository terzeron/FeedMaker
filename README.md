FeedMaker
=========

Utility set of making feeds from websites

Requirements
------------
1. Python & modules
  1. bs4
1. Perl & modules
  1. Digest::MD5
  1. HTML::Parser
  1. HTML::TreeBuilder::XPath
  1. JSON
  1. Modern::Perl
  1. Scalar::Util
  1. Text::Levenshtein
  1. URI::Encode
  1. XML::RSS
  1. XML::Simple	

Usage
-----

1. cd feedmaker
1. . bin/setup.sh
1. cd \<sitedir\>/\<feeddir\>
1. run.sh
